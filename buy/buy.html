Payments

Shopping is fun, and everyone loves it. But, getting your credit or debit card details or typing in an OTP every time you make a purchase might get tiring. Smart-Digital Pay Later now introduces the Shop Now Pay Later experience using which you can select the products you want, check out, and receive the products without having to scramble for payment details or OTPs. You can just pay for all your products whenever it’s convenient for you in one go. You also get a single bill for all your purchases, making it easy for you to track your expenses. So what if you’re way past your budget for the month. Now, you can get the products you need at your convenience after you’ve experienced them.

Login to Smart-Digital.com (Mobile App/Mobile Site/Website) Add the required products to your cart Proceed to checkout Select UPI payment option Offer discount applied automatically (You will have the discount summary) Complete your transaction using your any of your favorite UPI applicationsOffer is applicable on all products except Flight, bus, Gold coins, Grocery, Supermarket Refurbished, and any product which should not be incentivized as per Government regulation. What are the other conditions to avail the Offer? Offer is applicable on the first UPI payment transaction. Offer can be availed only once during the Offer Period. Once you add all required items to your cart & proceed to check out by selecting UPI payment Instrument, your order summary will be updated automatically with a discounted amount.

We accept payments made by credit/debit cards issued in India and 21 other countries.
Credit card: We accept payments made using Visa, MasterCard and American Express credit cards.
Debit card: We accept payments made using Visa, MasterCard and Maestro debit cards.
Internationally issued credit/debit cards cannot be used for Flyte, Wallet and eGV payments/top-ups.

NetBanking is a payment method that you can use to make manual payments using a bank transfer. This kind of payment is supported by our processors. Requirements and security: You can use NetBanking to make a manual payment if-You have a billing address and bank account in India, Your purchase currency is Indian Rupee (INR), Your bank is supported by our processors.

If you are not comfortable making an online payment on Smart-Digital.com, you can opt for the Cash on Delivery (C-o-D) payment method instead. With C-o-D you can pay in cash at the time of actual delivery of the product at your doorstep, without requiring you to make any advance payment online.

There is NO processing fee charged for availing Smart-Digital's EMI payment option. On return or exchange, interest charged by the bank till that time will not be refunded by Smart-Digital. You may check with the respective bank/issuer on how a cancellation, refund or pre-closure could affect the EMI terms, and what interest charges would be levied on you for the same.
